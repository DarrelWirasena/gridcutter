Privacy Policy for GridCutter

Last updated: April 2026

At GridCutter, accessible from gridcutter.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by GridCutter and how we use it.

Client-Side Processing (Your Data is Safe)

Crucial Information: GridCutter is a client-side application. This means that any images you upload to be cropped are processed entirely within your own web browser. We do not upload, store, or transmit your images to our servers. Your personal media remains on your device at all times.

Log Files

GridCutter follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to GridCutter and other sites on the internet.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors engage with GridCutter. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information and report website trends without identifying individual visitors. The data collected includes information such as your device type, browser, and pages visited. This helps us improve the user experience and performance of our tool.

You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use our site by visiting How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
