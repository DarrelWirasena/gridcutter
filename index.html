FAQ

Common questions about GridCutter.

Who is GridCutter for?

GridCutter is for anyone who wants a seamless Instagram grid without doing the manual math. Whether you are managing a personal profile or a professional feed, it is specifically built for users who want to maintain the traditional 4:5 aspect ratio for maximum feed visibility, while ensuring perfect alignment with Instagram's new, taller 3:4 profile grid.

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. GridCutter processes images entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your image is never sent to any server."

What file types are supported?

GridCutter currently supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP images.

What grid sizes can I make?

GridCutter includes quick presets for the most common layouts: 3×1, 3×2, and 3×3. If you need a massive continuous feed, you can open the Advanced settings to manually create custom layouts up to 3×6 (18 tiles). Note: Because GridCutter processes everything securely on your own device without uploading to a server, generating very large grids may take a second or two to render.

Why is GridCutter different from other grid cutters?

It was built to avoid two common problems: incorrect cutting logic and ugly solid-color buffer areas that appear when some tools try to fake alignment.

What is the correct posting order?

For multi-tile grids, post from the bottom-right tile back toward the top-left so the finished layout lands correctly on your profile.

Does GridCutter work on mobile?

GridCutter is designed to work in modern browsers on desktop and mobile. The best experience is on up-to-date browsers.

Is GridCutter free?

Yes, the core tool is free to use and requires no account or subscription.
